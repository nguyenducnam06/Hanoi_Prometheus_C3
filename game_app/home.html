About Zero-Waste Kitchen

The Zero-Waste Kitchen is a short, web game designed to raise awareness about the environmental and financial impact of Wasting Food. You step into the role of a household manager tasked with maximising resources and minimising spoilage over a three-day cycle. The game challenges you across three stages: Smart Shopping (Run to collect ingredients for your kitchen), Meal Planning (using purchased ingredients efficiently), and the Leftover Merge Puzzle (merging the waste food to create new dishes). Your goal is to keep the Waste Score as low as possible, highlighting how conscious choices in the kitchen directly benefit both your wallet and the planet.

Developers

Developed by the Prometheus team during the RMIT Hackathon 2025, this game combines engaging gameplay with educational content to inspire players to adopt sustainable food practices in their daily lives.
